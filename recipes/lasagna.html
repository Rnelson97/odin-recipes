Lasagna Recipe

Prepare to savor the ultimate comfort food with this classic lasagna recipe. Layers of tender pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and a generous sprinkling of cheese come together to create a hearty Italian masterpiece that's sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves and parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium-high heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, cooking until they're softened and fragrant.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 10 minutes, then remove from heat.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese and beaten egg. Mix well.
  5. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, start assembling the lasagna: begin with a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, a layer of ricotta mixture, and a generous sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat this layering process until all ingredients are used up, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes or until the lasagna is bubbling and the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let it rest for about 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil and parsley if desired. Enjoy!
