Chocolate Chip Pancakes Recipe

Indulge in a delightful breakfast treat with these fluffy chocolate chip pancakes. These mouthwatering pancakes are a perfect blend of light, airy batter and rich, melty chocolate chips, making each bite a heavenly experience.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • Additional chocolate chips for topping (optional)
  • Maple syrup or whipped cream for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the egg and then add buttermilk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.
  4. Gently fold in the chocolate chips into the batter.
  5. Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium-high heat and lightly grease it with butter or cooking spray.
  6. Using a ladle or measuring cup, pour a portion of the batter onto the skillet to form pancakes of your desired size.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown. This should take about 2-3 minutes per side.
  8. Remove the pancakes from the skillet and keep them warm while you cook the remaining batter.
  9. Serve the chocolate chip pancakes hot, topped with additional chocolate chips, maple syrup, or a dollop of whipped cream if desired. Enjoy!
